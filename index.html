IMMER

This was my final project for Harvard's CS50 course. It is a language immersion tool developed to help people find content in their target language. It takes a search query, translates it and scrapes the web for translated results. The idea is to help make it easier for beginners to make progress and bridge the gap to the intermediate language levels when they may not have a robust vocabulary to find content on their own yet.

Essayez-le ! J'espère que vous aimes!

This is a project I intend to keep growing and I also hope to create a few more like it as what might be described as a language learners toolkit.

BLACK CAT VINYL

Black Cat Vinyl is a pop up record store. Many of the features on this site are similar to the features on Blankc Guitars. Both are also local to Kansas City and decided they wanted that enhanced in their branding. In addition to the inventory tracking, this site allows people who sign up for an account to share their favorite lyrics from songs which are pooled together and randomly displayed at the lyrics page.
